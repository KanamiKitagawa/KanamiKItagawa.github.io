About Kanami
Biography
Kanami Kitagawa is an undergraduate student at the University of California, Berkeley, who has a passion for nutrition, health, and well-being. Through her time spent in New Zealand, Japan, and America, she has gained multi-cultural experiences and insights into the food systems and habits of these respective cultures. She hopes to be able to apply these in her work towards creating a more health-conscious environment in whichever community she serves.
Work Philosophy
Health as a Priority: I believe in the need to make health a priority for everyone, by making the means and knowledge more accessible to any population. At the same time, there are individuals that need more immediate care and attention, and my role as a future Registered Dietitian will be to serve individuals in their journey to achieving a more healthful lifestyle.
